Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines designed to think and act like humans.

Key Areas of AI

Machine Learning
Machine Learning allows computers to learn from data without being explicitly programmed.
Natural Language Processing
Natural Language Processing focuses on enabling computers to understand and interact using human language.
Robotics
Robotics combines AI with mechanical engineering to create intelligent machines capable of performing tasks.
